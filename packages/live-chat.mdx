 `Live Chat` allowing your users to communicate with you in real-time. We provides a simple way to integrate live chat into your application.

 ## Crisp

 [Crisp](https://crisp.chat/) is Your AI-powered inbox for modern customer support.

 Simple add `NEXT_PUBLIC_CRISP_WEBSITE_ID` environment variable to your project.

 ## UserDesk

 [UserDesk](https://userdesk.io/) is a no-code solution to create ChatGPT-like bots, trained on your website, Notion, PDFs, and more.

  Simple add `NEXT_PUBLIC_USERDESK_SITE_ID` and `NEXT_PUBLIC_USERDESK_SITE_SRC` environment variable to your project.
